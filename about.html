Our Story

From a bold idea between friends to building a startup aimed at delivering impactful digital solutions for modern businesses.

Your Trusted Technology Partner

Korzyn Consulting is a full-service technology and consulting startup focused on helping early-stage startups and small businesses succeed in the digital era. We specialize in building scalable SaaS platforms, AI-powered automation, custom CRM and internal tools, performance marketing strategies, and comprehensive e-commerce ecosystems.

Founded in 2025, we’ve started our journey as a small and passionate team of developers and entrepreneurs. Though we're new, our focus is clear to deliver real business value through smart and reliable digital solutions.

Our growing team also offers seamless integration services to unlock your business’s true potential. Whether you're a lean startup or a growing venture, we partner with you to boost innovation, efficiency, and digital growth.
